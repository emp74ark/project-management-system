What is a kanban board?

A kanban board is an agile project management tool designed to help visualize work, limit work-in-progress, and maximize efficiency (or flow). It can help both agile and DevOps teams establish order in their daily work. Kanban boards use cards, columns, and continuous improvement to help technology and service teams commit to the right amount of work, and get it done! By dividing the project into small tasks, you simplify the work process and the ability to divide the project between employees of different levels, capabilities and qualifications.
